Test Case ENTER-030
Title Pressing Enter when there is no Ticker Symbol
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When the enter button is pressed, rtnv shall do nothing if the ticker field is empty.

The objective of this test case is to verify the requirements identified above which cover the entry of a "null" stock into the portfolio.
Setup
  • Test case ENTER-020 was immediately executed prior to this test case.
Actions
  1. Press the "Enter" button.
  2. Verify a "blank" screen is displayed.
  3. Press the "Next" button and verify that the symbol AAPL is displayed with 200 shares and that a non zero value is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  4. Press the "Next" button again and verify that the symbol INTC is displayed with 500 shares and that a non zero value is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  5. Press the "Next" button again and verify that the symbol AAA is displayed with 1 share and that "Not found" is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  6. Press the "Next" button again and verify that a "blank" screen is displayed.
Expected Pressing enter with no stock ticker does nothing.
Results PASSED
Cleanup
  • Unnecessary
Requirements RTNV-PR-ENTER-020
